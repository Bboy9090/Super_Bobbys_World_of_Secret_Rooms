# Workshop-Certified Technician™ Program

## Purpose

Create industry trust through verified skill, judgment, and compliance.

---

## Levels

### Level I — Diagnostic Steward
- Device analysis
- Ownership verification
- Legal classification
- Audit discipline

### Level II — Repair Custodian
- Screen, battery, port replacement
- Guided repair compliance
- Customer transparency

### Level III — Interpretive Authority
- Custodian Vault access
- High-risk scenario handling
- Documentation review
- External authority routing

---

## Verification

- Hardware-bound exams
- Logged assessments
- Non-transferable credentials

---

## Revocation

Certification revoked for:
- Misrepresentation of ownership
- Attempted circumvention
- Audit evasion

---

**Certification is trust, not access.**
