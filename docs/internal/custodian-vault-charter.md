# Bobby's Secret Room™ — The Custodian Vault Charter

**Mode**: Interpretive (Analysis-Only)

---

## Charter Statement

The Custodian Vault exists to analyze ownership, legality, risk, and precedent—
and to route lawful recovery through appropriate authorities.
It does not perform circumvention.

---

## Access Requirements

- ✅ Verified user identity
- ✅ Proof of ownership or documented authority
- ✅ Jurisdiction detection
- ✅ Hardware serial handshake
- ✅ Permanent audit logging

---

## What the Vault May Do

✅ **Permitted Functions**:
- Classify scenarios (clear / conditional / prohibited)
- Map applicable laws and exemptions (by region)
- Identify required external authorizations
- Produce compliance-ready documentation
- Require human sign-off for high-risk cases

---

## What the Vault Never Does

❌ **Prohibited Functions**:
- Execute or automate bypasses
- Provide procedural steps
- Link to binaries or scripts
- Allow silent actions

---

## Escalation Paths (Routing, Not Doing)

- OEM request flows
- Carrier eligibility checks
- Notarized affidavits
- Executor documentation
- Court order references

---

## Audit & Oversight

- Every interaction is logged
- Timestamped and attributable
- Immutable and exportable
- No silent paths

---

**The Custodian Vault is wisdom, not capability.**
