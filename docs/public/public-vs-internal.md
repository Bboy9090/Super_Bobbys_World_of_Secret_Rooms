# Public vs Internal Boundary

## ✅ Public (Ships)

- Analysis and classification
- Legal pathways and disclosures
- Reports and audit logs
- Education and policy summaries
- Routing guidance
- UI/UX copy
- Certification systems

---

## ❌ Internal (Never Ships)

- Exploit execution knowledge
- Grey-zone tactics
- Tool-specific operations
- Automation concepts
- Bypass workflows
- Step-by-step instructions

---

## Rule

**If it executes, it is internal.**

---

## Pandora Codex Status

**Pandora Codex is INTERNAL ONLY.**

It may:
- Inform classification logic
- Influence risk scoring
- Shape interpretive language

It may NEVER:
- Surface steps
- Surface tools
- Surface instructions
- Surface automation

---

**Boundaries are non-negotiable.**
